Oscars Website - Projects

Text Adventure

Using java, I created a mini treasure quest. A player is supposed to wander around the world, depending on the choices he makes. It can either lead to another place in the world, or leading to a monster. You must defeat the monster, or you will die. You must survive till the end, if you reach the end, you will complete the quest and recieve treasure.

Recreation of Pong Game

Pong Game is a table tennis game where two players are allowed to play it using Java. The two players each control their own paddle, and with a ball bouncing, you must hit it to the other side to get points. The person with the most points wins.

Wealth Distribution

Wealth Distribution Lab reads from a .csv file that has income data. It gets separated by year and becomes organized into a two-dimensional array. You are allowed to find many topics from that year, the country, and the population. It outputs any data that is there, and can also output the Gini index of that year.

Greenfoot

One of my first ever projects in computer science. I had to create any project that I wanted. Using the arrow keys, you are supposed to dodge the objects that come out of the spaceship within the time limit. If you get hit with it, the game ends.If you manage to survive, you win.

All-Star Market

Working with a partner, we created an NBA All-Star market. Dividing the 2023 All-Stars depending on the conference, you are allowed to find all stars on either one. Putting them in your roster, or a 2D array, you are allowed to have a max of 5 players. When you reach the max, it will print your roster and the average amount, and ask if you would like to trade players.
